Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 12, 2026
HIPAA Notice: Snapback Health Technologies LLC is committed to protecting your Protected Health Information (PHI) in accordance with the Health Insurance Portability and Accountability Act (HIPAA). This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and protect your health information.

1. Who We Are

Snapback Health Technologies LLC ("Snapback Health," "we," "us," or "our") is an Arizona limited liability company that provides a patient recovery portal for orthopedic surgery patients. We partner with healthcare clinics to deliver personalized post-operative care support through our web application at app.snapbackhealth.com.

Contact us at: hello@snapbackhealth.com

2. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information when you use our platform:

  • Account information: Name, email address, and password
  • Health information (PHI): Procedure type, surgery date, pain scores, temperature readings, wound photos, medication lists, and activity logs
  • Contact information: Mobile phone number (if you opt in to SMS reminders)
  • Survey responses: KOOS, HOOS, and other patient-reported outcome (PRO) survey answers
  • Usage data: How you interact with the application, device type, and browser information

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information solely to provide and improve our patient recovery services:

  • Delivering your personalized recovery portal, exercise program, and daily checklists
  • Sending medication reminders, exercise reminders, and recovery check-in notifications via email and SMS (only if you opt in)
  • Sharing your recovery progress and outcome survey results with your authorized healthcare provider
  • Analyzing de-identified, aggregated data to improve our services
  • Complying with CMS and Medicare reporting requirements for applicable procedures

4. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell your personal information or health data to any third party. We share your information only in the following limited circumstances:

  • Your healthcare provider: Your clinic and care team can view your recovery data, readings, and survey results through our provider portal
  • Service providers (Business Associates): We share data with vendors who help us operate our platform, each under a signed Business Associate Agreement (BAA) as required by HIPAA. These include our database provider (Supabase), hosting provider (Vercel), and email provider (Resend)
  • Legal requirements: We may disclose information if required by law or to protect the rights and safety of our users

5. SMS / Text Message Reminders

If you choose to receive SMS reminders, you explicitly consent to receive automated text messages from Snapback Health at the mobile number you provide. Message frequency varies based on your reminder settings. Message and data rates may apply.

To opt out of SMS reminders at any time:

  • Reply STOP to any text message, or
  • Turn off SMS reminders in your account settings under the Reminders tab

For help, reply HELP to any message or email us at hello@snapbackhealth.com.

We do not share your mobile number with third parties for marketing purposes.

6. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your health information:

  • All data is encrypted in transit using TLS/HTTPS
  • All data is encrypted at rest in our database
  • Access to patient data is controlled by Row Level Security — each patient can only access their own records
  • Healthcare provider access is limited to patients at their own clinic
  • We sign Business Associate Agreements with all vendors who handle PHI

7. Data Retention

We retain your health information for as long as your account is active or as required by applicable law. You may request deletion of your account and associated data by contacting us at hello@snapbackhealth.com. Note that certain records may be retained as required by HIPAA and applicable state law.

8. Your Rights

Under HIPAA and applicable law, you have the right to:

  • Access and receive a copy of your health information
  • Request corrections to your health information
  • Request restrictions on how your information is used or shared
  • Receive an accounting of disclosures of your health information
  • File a complaint with the U.S. Department of Health and Human Services if you believe your privacy rights have been violated

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at hello@snapbackhealth.com.

9. Children's Privacy

Our platform is intended for adult patients (18 years and older). We do not knowingly collect information from individuals under 18. If you believe a minor has provided us with health information, please contact us immediately.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by email or through a notice in the patient portal. Your continued use of our services after changes take effect constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
