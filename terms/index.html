2. Description of Service

Snapback Health provides a web-based patient recovery portal for post-surgical and post-procedural patients. The Service includes:

  • Personalized recovery tracking, education content, and daily checklists
  • Exercise programs and activity logging
  • Medication reminders and health metric logging (vital signs, pain, mood, sleep)
  • AI-powered nurse chat for general recovery questions (with PHI sanitization applied before transmission to our AI provider)

  • Patient-reported outcome surveys (KOOS Jr., HOOS Jr., QuickDASH, PROMIS, and others)
  • Appointment scheduling, tracking, and reminders
  • Email and SMS recovery reminders (opt-in only)
