SMS Reminder Program

Snapback Health Recovery Reminders is an opt-in SMS program that sends post-surgical and post-procedural patients automated reminders for medication, exercises, wound care, daily check-ins, and appointments. This page describes the program, how patients opt in, and how they can opt out.

How Patients Opt In to SMS

Patients can only enroll in Snapback Health after being invited by their healthcare provider (clinic). When a patient is referred to Snapback Health, the opt-in process works as follows:

1Patient receives clinic code from their care team

The clinic provides each patient with a short alphanumeric clinic code (for example: OSI, FBJ, or CARDIAC) during their pre-operative visit or via secure email.

2Patient creates account at app.snapbackhealth.com

During account creation, the patient provides their name, email, password, and the clinic code provided by their care team. They review and check the consent box shown below before their account is created.

Required consent during signup
✓
I consent to use Snapback Health as a remote therapeutic monitoring tool prescribed by my care team. I understand my care team will review my data to support my recovery. I have read the Privacy Policy and Terms of Service.

Note: This checkbox is required to create an account. Patients cannot proceed without affirmatively checking this box. The consent is logged with timestamp in our database.

3Patient affirmatively enables SMS for specific reminders

After account creation, the patient navigates to the Reminders page in the portal. SMS is disabled by default for all reminder types. The patient must affirmatively toggle SMS on for each reminder type they wish to receive via text message, and provide their mobile phone number.

Example of the Reminders settings page in the app:

🏃

📸

Patients control every reminder. Each reminder type has independent SMS and email toggles. Patients can enable SMS for some reminder types and not others. They can also customize the time of day each reminder is delivered. Patients can change these settings at any time.

Program Details

  • Program name: Snapback Health Recovery Reminders
  • Description: Automated medication reminders, exercise reminders, wound photo reminders, daily check-in reminders, and appointment reminders for post-surgical and post-procedural patients
  • Message frequency: Varies based on patient settings — up to 6 messages per day
  • Charges: Message and data rates may apply
  • Eligible patients: Patients invited by a participating healthcare clinic who have created a Snapback Health account and affirmatively enabled SMS reminders

How to Opt Out

Patients can stop receiving SMS messages at any time using any of the following methods:

  • Reply STOP to any text message — opt-out is immediate
  • Reply HELP to any message for support information
  • Sign in to the patient portal, go to the Reminders page, and toggle SMS off for any or all reminder types
  • Email hello@snapbackhealth.com to request removal from all SMS communications

Privacy and Data Handling

Snapback Health is a HIPAA-compliant healthcare technology platform. We do not share patient mobile phone numbers or SMS opt-in data with any third parties or affiliates for marketing or promotional purposes under any circumstances. Mobile information is used solely for delivering the recovery reminders patients have explicitly opted in to receive.

Complete details of our data handling practices are in our Privacy Policy and Terms of Service.
